\subsection*{Key Findings:}

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Best result:} APBF at 3h = \textbf{144.89 kW} (54\% better than baseline!)
\item \textbf{Beats baseline:} APBF 3h (144.89) and APBF 6h (211.87) both beat GRU (315.82)
\item \textbf{Strategy effectiveness:}
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Advanced beats Naive at 3h (298.92 vs 336.68) = 11\% improvement
  \item Advanced beats Naive at 6h (280.28 vs 582.23) = 52\% improvement
  \item APBF dramatically improves over Advanced at 3h (144.89 vs 298.92) = 52\% improvement
  \end{itemize}
\item \textbf{Horizon difficulty:} 3h easiest, 48h hardest (errors increase with forecast length)
\item \textbf{APBF limitation:} Failed at 48h due to LLM inability to generate exactly 288 values
\end{itemize}
